WHAT HAPPENS IN A CST SESSION?
The practitioner will take your medical history and asses your current condition.
Thereafter you will lie fully-clothed, face up or on your side , on a treatment table.
Using a light touch the practictioner will 'listen' to asses the potency of your craniosacral system ( the membranes, fluids, tissues and skeletal bones that surround the brain and spinal cord).
Sessions normally last an hour.
