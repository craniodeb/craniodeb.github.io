All about symptoms that Cranio fixes!

CST has been found to be beneficial for a wide variety of conditions, ranging from anxiety to arthritis and asthma and many more.
Given the stress of modern life, CST offers an opportunity for the body to release stress before it leads to chronic illness or discomfort.

Due to the gentle nature of CST and its focus on CREATING AND MAINTAINING A HEALTHY AND BALANCED STATE OF WELL BEING, most people will benefit from one or a series of CST sessions.

It is suitable for people of all ages from newborn babies to the elderly.

If you are in doubt about whether a condition is appropriate for CST, please contact the practitioner or the CST Association of South Africa (CSTASA).
